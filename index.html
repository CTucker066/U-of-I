The Idaho Assistive Technology Project (IATP) works to increase the availability of assistive technology for older persons and Idahoans with disabilities.

This federally funded program is located at the Center on Disabilities and Human Development through the University of Idaho. We provide devices, services and resources that help people receive the assistive technology they need. Learn how we can help you achieve your goals for a more independent lifestyle.

Consultation and training

We have the staff and resources available to meet your assistive technology needs. We hold regular ‘for credit’, conduct workshops and presentations for individuals and organizations, and provide on-going technical assistance throughout Idaho.
